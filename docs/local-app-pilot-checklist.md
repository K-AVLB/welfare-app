# Local App Pilot Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist is for internal pilot use of the local welfare recommendation app.

## Before Use

- Confirm the app is marked as `internal pilot` or `reference only`.
- Confirm the current data export date.
- Confirm the person responsible for data updates.
- Confirm the device is managed and access-controlled.

## During Use

- Enter the minimum amount of case detail needed.
- Do not enter resident numbers, exact addresses, full names, or other direct identifiers.
- Treat recommendation results as reference only.
- Verify eligibility, age range, and current conditions from the detail link or the responsible agency before guidance.

## Operational Rules

- Assign one owner for data refresh.
- Refresh local data when welfare programs are changed.
- Keep a simple version history of exported data.
- Separate this tool from official record systems.

## User Notice

Recommended notice:

`본 도구는 학생 상담 지원을 위한 내부 시범운영용 참고 시스템입니다. 추천 결과는 참고용이며, 최종 판단과 안내는 담당자가 직접 확인해야 합니다.`

## Stop Conditions

- Program data is outdated and not refreshed.
- The tool begins to be treated as an automatic decision system.
- Personal data is being entered beyond minimum operational need.
